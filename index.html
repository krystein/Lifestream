    Our Beliefs And Practices

    We are a non-denominational church with a high view of the person, work, and path of Jesus. We have been saved by grace through faith, which is itself a gift. That gift empowers us to be a transformed people who embody His redemptive power and participate in His renewal of all things. Therefore, all of life is all for Jesus.

    Our Outward Focus

    We are a sent people. As the church we are not called to turn inward and build our own kingdom. We are called to turn outward and give ourselves to the spread of the Gospel, the establishment of healthy local churches, and the multiplication of disciples. This is a call to invest beyond our church, beyond our tribe, and beyond our generation.

    OUR CONGREGATIONS

    OUR LEADERSHIP STRUCTURE

    We are multi-congregational. A central governing body provides structure, direction, and support for the ministry of each local congregation. Local congregations are united and collaborative, but each has its own leadership and is free to contextualize its efforts. As much as possible, we look to empower decision-making at the local level.
